BackGround
----------------
NCDs account for 63 % of mortality globally and the 80 % of NCD-related deaths that occur in low and middle income countries

Out of the many NCDs, four – cardiovascular diseases, diabetes, cancers and chronic respiratory illnesses-have been identified as being responsible for the majority (75 %) of all NCD-related mortality




These four NCD also share a set of four Risk Factors:
--------------------------------------------------------
tobacco use, unhealthy diet, harmful alcohol use and physical inactivity.
Age factor from childhood to old age

The social determinants of health are those conditions in which people are born, grow, work, 
live, and age, and the wider set of forces and systems shaping the conditions of daily life


WHO recommended “best buys” for NCD prevention and control


Tobacco use : Raise taxes on tobacco,Protect people from tobacco smoke,Warn about the dangers of tobacco,Enforce bans on tobacco advertising
